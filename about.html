about me

My name is Terry Migwi and I help organisations solve real world problems using data. I believe data is an important asset that organisations should leverage while making decisions. I am originally from Kiambu County, Kenya. As a Data Scientist, I have engaged in various projects that have exposed me to different Data Science fields such as Data Visualization, Machine Learning and Computer Vision. I enjoy interacting with people and having conversations around real life. From my interaction with different people, I have come to better appreciate diverse thought processes and differences in opinion hence find it easier to relate with people from different walks of life. I have a passion to mentor young people and my goal is to help them reach their full potential.
